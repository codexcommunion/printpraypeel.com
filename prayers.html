Each prayer is a JSON file with helpful metadata (id, category, source, text). Install and import directly:

import hailMary from '@codexcommunion/prayer-collection/prayers/core/hail-mary.json';

üôè Some Featured Prayers

A sampling of frequently used prayers across our sticker themes and devotional projects:

Glory Be

A short hymn of praise to the Trinity, also known as the Gloria Patri or Lesser Doxology

Hail Mary

A traditional Catholic prayer to the Virgin Mary, combining the angel Gabriel's greeting and Elizabeth's blessing

Our Father

The prayer taught by Jesus Christ to his disciples, also known as the Lord's Prayer

Apostles' Creed

A statement of Christian faith summarizing the core beliefs of Christianity, organized around the three persons of the Trinity

The Angelus

A traditional Marian devotion commemorating the Annunciation, recited three times daily

Hail Holy Queen (Salve Regina)

A Marian antiphon expressing devotion to Mary as Queen of Heaven and Mother of Mercy

Memorare

A traditional prayer to the Virgin Mary asking for her intercession, expressing confidence in her maternal care

Act of Contrition

A prayer expressing sorrow for sins committed and firm purpose of amendment, commonly recited during confession

Prayer to St. Michael the Archangel

A prayer for protection against evil, invoking the intercession of St. Michael the Archangel
